Gem Game

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are 5 gems. By clicking on a gem you will modify your score.

You win the game by matching your score to the random number, you lose the game if your score goes above the random number.

The valube of each gem is hidden from you until you click on it

Each time the game starts, the value of the gems will change
